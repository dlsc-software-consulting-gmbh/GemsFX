### PowerPane

The "PowerPane" combines several other highly specialized panes of GemsFX and
ControlsFX. They are:

- InfoCenterPane - to display notifications</li>
- DialogPane - to display dialogs</li>
- DrawerStackPane - to display a drawer / a tray that slides in from the bottom</li>
- HiddenSidesPane - to display trays sliding in from either one of the four sides</li>

This pane is a good candidate to be used as the basis for a rich client application.
